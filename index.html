About Us

RedBog renewables is an energy service provider for a wide range of renewable technologies on the island of Ireland, including wind, solar and hydro.

Established as new start up in 2020, RedBog aims to help provide essential services to manage and maintain the growing green energy portfolio in Ireland.

The small team based in County Meath have over twenty years of experience in the renewable field. The team ranges from design engineers to financial analysts
